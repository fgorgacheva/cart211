When you abandon a pet, their worlds shatter. Everything they'd known until then disappears into a mess of confusion and betrayal. Each year, thousands of pets are abandonned in the wild to suffer from sicknesses, diseases, starvation and even death. Animals are not toys, they are living and breathing beings with beating hearts.
If you wouldn't abandon a child, you shouldn't abandon an animal.
They wouldn't abandon you.
