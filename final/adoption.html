Adoption Saves Lives!

If you are willing and capable of taking in an animal and are looking to help this situation more proactively, adopting an animal from a shelter is one of the most precious things one can do. The animals who've ended up in shelters seek nothing more but to be loved again, all they need is another chance.

By adopting an animal from a shelter, they are saved from potential euthanasia and they are given the opportunity to once again be loyal to someone, be cuddled and cared for. This especially goes for the elders and for those with disabilities. The old animals may not have much longer left, but adopting them will allow them to live a good life for the little time they have left. As for special needs animals, they require more care but they offer just as much love and joy as the others.

Look into your neighborhood shelters and rescues and inform yourself on the animals that have been surrendered! You could save a life!
