Do cats and dogs feel any emotions?

"[Cats and dogs] show the six primary, or basic, emotions: anger, happiness, fear, surprise, disgust, and sadness. These are the same emotions which show up reliably in human children by the time they are around two and a half years of age." Which emotions do we see in dogs and cats? Stanley Coren PhD., Psychology Today July 14, 2016

"[...] Pets can experience severe health impacts from abandonment. [...] Many pets will starve or suffer adverse health effects from malnutrition [...].
